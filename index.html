Frequently Asked Questions

What makes CleanType different from other text editors?

CleanType is built with a focus on distraction-free writing, combining minimalist design with powerful features. Built with Rust and Tauri, it offers superior performance while using minimal system resources.

Is CleanType free to use?

Yes, CleanType is completely free and open-source. You can use it for personal or commercial purposes without any restrictions.

Can I customize the appearance?

Absolutely! CleanType offers various themes, fonts, and layout options that you can customize to match your preferences and writing style.

What platforms does CleanType support?

Currently, CleanType is available for Windows. Support for macOS and Linux is planned for future releases.
